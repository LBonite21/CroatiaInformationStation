Restaurants and Local Foods

Pelegrini

Three consecutive years in a row, Pelegrini has been crowned Croatia's best restaurant, with the resident chef. Rudolf Stefan also winning several best chef awards. Put simply, Pelegrini is outstanding and at the cutting edge of Croatian cuisine. The food here will blow your mind and looks as good as it tastes. Be sure to book well in advance. The view of the UNESCO-listed cathedral is also a big seller.

Dubravkin Put

Located in the woods a short walk from Zagreb, you'll find Dubravkin Put. It's here that you will find stylish and sophistecated Medditerranean cuisine served to you in style. The restaurant has one of Croatia's largest wine lists, with many of those by the glass making your experience at theis restaurant even better.
